# Art direction: bauhaus-playful

Palette tendencies:
- Warm off-white base with primary red, cobalt blue, mustard yellow, and black.
- Use color in clear blocks; reserve strong contrast for hierarchy and calls to action.

Composition:
- Grid-led layouts with playful interruption from circles, bars, and angled accents.
- Balance strict alignment with one surprising geometric moment per screen.

Typography:
- Clean geometric sans serif.
- Tight headline rhythm, explicit scale jumps, crisp labels.

Imagery:
- Geometric abstraction, modular diagrams, simplified object silhouettes, and icon-like motifs.
- Prefer constructed forms over illustration detail.

Motion:
- Snaps, slides, wipes, and measured rotations.
- Transitions should feel precise and rhythmic, not bouncy.

Forbidden clichés:
- Overcrowded poster replicas, fake print distress, random rainbow palettes, ornamental gradients.

UI-control discipline:
- Interactive controls should read as deliberate system elements, not decoration.
- Use geometry to clarify affordances, not multiply them.
- Remove any geometric accent that could be mistaken for a button without a matching interaction entry.
